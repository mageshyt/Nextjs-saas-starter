# Welcome to NextJS SAAS Starter

NextJS SAAS Starter is an **open-source boilerplate** designed to accelerate your SaaS development journey. Built with modern technologies and best practices, it provides everything you need to launch your SaaS product quickly and efficiently.

## What's Included

- 🚀 **Next.js 14** - The React framework for production
- 🔐 **Auth.js v5** - Authentication with multiple providers
- 💳 **Stripe Integration** - Ready-to-use payment processing
- 🎨 **Shadcn/ui** - Beautiful and accessible UI components
- 🗃️ **Prisma & Neon** - Type-safe database operations

## Why Choose This Starter?

- Launch your SaaS product faster
- Focus on building features, not infrastructure
- Follow best practices out of the box
- Scale with confidence
